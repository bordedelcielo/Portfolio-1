About the teacher

Hi, I'm Corri, and I am the owner and teacher of Long Tones Music.

I have been teaching music lessons for about a decade.
I studied Jazz Voice at Mohawk College. I have completed up to Level 8 in RCM Voice and Level 6 in RCM piano.

voice & piano lessons

Voice lessons:
Voice lessons are offered for all styles and needs ages 8 and up. I do not accept students under the age of 8 due the fact that their voices are still developing.

Piano lessons:
Piano lessons are offered for all styles and needs ages 5 and up. Some acceptions may apply to younger students. Please contact me for more details.
