Hi, I'm Corri 👾

(Short for Corrigan)

I'm a highly skilled and creative web developer based in Ontario, Canada. I build and design user-friendly websites and applications that are inspired by art and nature, while also keeping accessabilty in mind.

I have been building websites since I was 12 years old. I started out using HTML and Flash with my first site on Geocities as well as customizing my Neopets store. Later, I began making MySpace layouts for all my friends.

Some of my skills include: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, SQL & NoSQL, Python, React, Node.js, Salesforce, Figma, Blender, and much more! I am always up for learning something new!
