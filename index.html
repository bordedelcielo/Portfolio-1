Hi, I'm Corri 👾

(Short for Corrigan)

I first started coding websites when I was 12 years old using HTML and Flash on Geocities and Neopets. Later, I began making custom MySpace layouts for all my friends because I had the experience to create unique and beautiful designs.

Now, I'm a highly skilled and creative web developer based in Ontario, Canada. I build user-friendly websites and applications that are inspired by art and nature, while also keeping accessabilty in mind.

Some of my skills include: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, SQL & NoSQL, Python, React, Node.js, Salesforce, Figma, Blender, and much more!
